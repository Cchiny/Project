Dataset

Data exploration

  • Youtube creators per countires
# Top 10 countires, excluding channel that have 'nan' in country column)
top5_countries <- sqldf("SELECT count(*) as number, country
           FROM Global_Youtube_Statistics
           WHERE country <> 'nan'
           GROUP by country
           ORDER BY number DESC
           LIMIT 5")
#plot
ggplot(data = top5_countries) +
  geom_bar(mapping = aes(x = country, y = number, fill = country),stat = "identity") + 
  labs(title = "Countries by number of Youtube channel",
       subtitle = "Top 5 Countries with Most Top 996 YouTube Channels",
       caption = "Data collected by Nidula Elgiriyewithana",
       x = "Country",
       y = "Number of channel")

Based on the bar graph, The United States has the most YouTube channels who are ranking in the top 996 worldwide, followed by India and Brazil

# Calculate the count of each category
category_count <- Global_Youtube_Statistics %>% 
  group_by(category) %>% 
  summarise(count = n())

ggplot(data = category_count) +
  geom_bar(mapping = aes(x = reorder(category, count), y = count ), stat = "identity") +
  coord_flip() +
  theme_bw()+
  labs(title = "Categories by number of Youtube channel",
       subtitle = "The most popular categories among the top 996 highest subscribed channels.",
       caption = "Data collected by Nidula Elgiriyewithana",
       x = "Category",
       y = "Count")

Based on the analysis of the top 996 highest subscribed YouTube channels, it is evident that the most prevalent category among these channels is entertainment fllowed by music and People & Blog. This information underscores the dominance of entertainment-related content on the platform

ggplot(data = Global_Youtube_Statistics) +
  geom_point(mapping = aes(x = subscribers, y = video.views)) +
  geom_smooth(mapping = aes(x = subscribers, y = video.views), method = "lm") +
  scale_x_continuous(labels = comma) +
  scale_y_continuous(labels = comma) +
  labs(title = "Subscibers per Total Video Views",
       subtitle = "Correlation between Youtube channel subscirbers and Total Views",
